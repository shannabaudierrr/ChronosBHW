About

I am a 20-year-old, second-year student at Louisiana State University. I am majoring in Art (BA) with a focus in graphic design and digital art. I am minoring in mass communications, focusing in advertising. I am on track to graduating in May of 2024.
I was born in Mississippi and raised in Louisiana. I have always had a strong passion for art and creations. Nothing brings me more pleasure than making nothing out of something. I love art, spending time with friends/family, and traveling. I can't wait to see what future of design brings to me! Enjoy the throwback picture.
